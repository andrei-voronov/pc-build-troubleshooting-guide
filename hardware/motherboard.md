# Motherboard

## Definition
The motherboard (mainboard) is the primary printed circuit board (PCB) of a computer.  
It connects all hardware components and allows them to communicate.

## Key Components
- CPU socket
- RAM slots (DIMM)
- Chipset
- PCIe slots
- SATA ports
- Power connectors (24-pin, 8-pin CPU)
- BIOS/UEFI chip

## Form Factors
- ATX
- Micro-ATX
- Mini-ITX

## Common Issues
- No POST
- No power
- Beep codes
- Physical damage (burn marks, swollen capacitors)

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Check power supply connections.
2. Reseat RAM.
3. Clear CMOS.
4. Inspect for physical damage.
5. Test with minimal hardware configuration.
